About

Before completing my education in graphic design, I had always been interested in art as an outsider. Encouraging myself to pursue a creative career has changed the lens I view the world.

Through my experience and interest in the material world, I draw on inspiration from pop and consumer culture, synthesising a modern design aesthetic. I aims to create considered and contemporary solutions for the modern brand.

I currently work at CHE Proximity as a Production Assistant.
