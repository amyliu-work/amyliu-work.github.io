About Amy

I'm a millenial that grew up consuming what I believe to be the golden age of the internet. I'm talking pixel art, talking to strangers in chat rooms and most definitely kicking my mum off the phone.

Before completing my diploma of graphic design, I have always been interested in design as an outsider. Encouraging myself to pursue a creative career has changed how I view the world and now I view most things through the lens of a designer.

I currently work at CHE Proximity as a Production Assistant. In my downtime, you can catch me hiding my snacks, giving pats to dogs and watering my 86 plants.
